Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our security services

What security services do you offer? ▼

We offer comprehensive security solutions including manned guarding, CCTV surveillance, alarm response, event security, VIP protection, and security consultancy. We specialize in serving schools, churches, corporate offices, and events across Nairobi.

Are your security personnel trained and vetted? ▼

Yes, all our security personnel undergo rigorous background checks, police clearance verification, and comprehensive security training. We maintain high standards of professionalism and discipline.

Do you provide 24/7 emergency response? ▼

Absolutely! Our emergency response teams are available 24/7. We maintain rapid response protocols to ensure immediate assistance during any security incident in Nairobi.

How do I apply for a job at FortSecure Kenya? ▼

We have a dedicated online careers portal where you can submit your application with required documents including National ID, KRA PIN, SHA certificate, NSSF certificate, CV, educational certificates, medical clearance, and police clearance.

What is your response time for urgent security matters? ▼

Our emergency response team aims to be on-site within 15-30 minutes for urgent matters in Nairobi. We maintain strategically positioned response units and mobile security teams for rapid deployment.

Can you customize security solutions for my business? ▼

Yes! We provide personalized security assessments and customized solutions tailored to your specific needs. Contact us for a free security consultation and risk evaluation.
